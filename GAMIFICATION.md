# NeighborGigs - Gamification Strategy

## Overview
Gamification transforms helping neighbors from a transactional chore into an engaging, rewarding experience. By layering points, achievements, leaderboards, and tangible rewards, we create intrinsic and extrinsic motivation that makes users excited to complete tasks‚Äîeven for minimal monetary compensation.

---

## Core Gamification Elements

### 1. Points System ("NeighborPoints")

**How Points Are Earned:**
- **Task completion**: 10-100 points (scaled by task difficulty/time)
  - Quick errand (grocery item): 10 points
  - Medium task (TV installation): 50 points
  - Major help (furniture moving): 100 points
- **Speed bonus**: +5-20 points for completing task ahead of deadline
- **First-time task category**: +25 points (encourages trying new task types)
- **Streak bonus**: +10 points per day for consecutive days with completed tasks
- **5-star rating received**: +15 points
- **Photo proof uploaded**: +5 points (quality assurance)
- **Helping first-time users**: +20 points (onboarding support)
- **Peak time completion**: +10 points (weekends, evenings when demand is high)

**Bonus Point Multipliers:**
- **2x points weekends**: Encourages weekend activity
- **Community events**: Special 3x point days (e.g., "Super Helper Saturday")
- **Referral bonus**: 50 points when referred friend completes first task

**Points Display:**
- Lifetime total points (never decrease)
- Monthly points (resets each month for monthly leaderboards)
- Points to next level/badge

### 8. Psychological Triggers

**Loss Aversion:**
- Streaks create fear of breaking momentum
- "Don't lose your 7-day streak!" notifications

**Variable Rewards:**
- Surprise bonus point drops (random 2x point tasks)
- Mystery badges unlock unexpectedly
- Unpredictability keeps users engaged

**Progress Visualization:**
- Circular progress bars for levels
- "You're 80% to the next badge!"
- Clear, visible progress = motivation

**Social Comparison:**
- "You're 15 points behind your neighbor Sarah!"
- Healthy competition drives action

**Autonomy & Mastery:**
- Users choose which categories to master
- Specialization paths (become the "Grocery King")
- Sense of ownership over their helper identity

**Immediate Feedback:**
- Points awarded instantly upon task completion
- Animated "+50 points!" pop-ups
- Dopamine hit reinforces behavior

## Gamification Metrics to Track

1. **Engagement Rate**: % of users completing 1+ task per week
2. **Streak Retention**: % of users maintaining 3/7/14-day streaks
3. **Leaderboard Participation**: % of users checking leaderboards weekly
4. **Point Redemption Rate**: How often users redeem points for rewards
5. **Badge Completion**: Average badges earned per user
6. **Challenge Completion**: % of daily/weekly challenges completed
7. **Social Shares**: # of milestone shares on social media
8. **Referral Rate**: # of new users from gamified referral program
